How We Began

Founded in 2008 by wedding planner Jennifer Royal, Royal Weddings started as a small event planning service with a single venue. Jennifer's vision was to create a space where couples could celebrate their love without the stress of coordinating multiple vendors.

What began as a modest operation has now grown into one of the most sought-after wedding destinations in the region, with three luxurious halls and a team of over 50 dedicated professionals.
